Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

OpenDoors ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you register for an account, we collect:

  • Name (first and last)
  • Email address
  • Date of birth (to verify you are 13 or older)
  • Password (encrypted)

Usage Information

We automatically collect information about your use of the app:

  • Game plays and results
  • Prizes won and redeemed
  • Ad viewing history (for earning doors)
  • Referral activity
  • App interactions and preferences

Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • Push notification tokens (if you grant permission)
  • App version and technical diagnostics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the OpenDoors service
  • Process game plays and prize redemptions
  • Send you transactional emails (account verification, password resets)
  • Send push notifications about earned rewards (if you opt in)
  • Track referrals and award bonus doors
  • Improve our app and user experience
  • Prevent fraud and ensure fair play

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With Businesses: When you redeem a prize, we share your name with the business to verify redemption
  • Service Providers: We use third-party services for hosting (Supabase), analytics, and advertising (Google AdMob)
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the app. AdMob may collect information about your device and ad interactions. You can learn more about Google's privacy practices at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted passwords
  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Row-level security on our database
  • Regular security updates

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Update or correct your information in your profile
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of push notifications
  • Request a copy of your data

7. Children's Privacy

OpenDoors is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your rights, contact us at:

Email: support@opendoorsgame.com
