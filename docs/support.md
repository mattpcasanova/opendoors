# OpenDoors Support

Welcome to OpenDoors support! We're here to help you win amazing prizes from local businesses.

## Getting Started

### How OpenDoors Works
1. **Get Plays**: Receive 1 free play daily, or get more by watching ads or through referrals
2. **Browse Games**: Explore games from local businesses in your area
3. **Play to Win**: Choose a door and play the game to try to win prizes
4. **Claim Rewards**: Show your QR code at the business to claim your prize

### Tutorial
New to OpenDoors? Launch the app and tap the tutorial button to take a guided tour of all features.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Account & Plays

**Q: How do I get more plays?**
A: You receive 1 free play every day. You can also:
- Watch video ads for additional plays
- Refer friends to earn bonus plays
- Receive gifted plays from businesses

**Q: When does my daily play reset?**
A: Your free daily play resets at midnight in your local timezone.

**Q: Can I save up plays?**
A: Yes! Unused plays accumulate and don't expire.

### Games & Prizes

**Q: How do I play a game?**
A: Tap on any game card to see details, then tap "Play Game" if you have plays available. Follow the on-screen instructions to choose doors and complete the game.

**Q: What are my odds of winning?**
A: Each business sets their own odds. You can see the prize details and game information before you play.

**Q: Can I play the same game multiple times?**
A: Yes! You can play any game as many times as you have plays available.

### Rewards

**Q: How do I claim my reward?**
A: Go to the Rewards tab, select your won prize, and show the QR code to the business. They will scan it to verify and give you your prize.

**Q: How long do I have to claim a reward?**
A: Rewards typically expire 30 days from when you win them. Check each reward for its specific expiration date.

**Q: What if the business won't accept my reward?**
A: Contact us immediately at support@opendoors-app.com with details, and we'll help resolve the issue.

### Location & Privacy

**Q: Why does OpenDoors need my location?**
A: We use your location to show you games from nearby businesses. You can enable/disable location services in your device settings.

**Q: Is my information private?**
A: Yes! Please read our [Privacy Policy](privacy.md) for complete details on how we protect your data.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The app isn't loading games**
A: Try these steps:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Enable location services for OpenDoors
3. Close and restart the app
4. Update to the latest version

**Q: I can't log in**
A:
1. Verify you're using the correct email/password
2. Try the "Forgot Password" option
3. Ensure you have a stable internet connection
4. Contact support if issues persist

**Q: The app crashes or freezes**
A:
1. Force quit and restart the app
2. Restart your device
3. Ensure you have the latest app version
4. Clear the app cache (in device settings)
