About OpenDoors

OpenDoors is a mobile app that lets you win real-world rewards from local businesses by playing engaging games. It's free to play, and you can earn "doors" (game plays) by watching ads, referring friends, or receiving them from partner businesses.

How It Works

1. Get Doors: Earn game plays by watching ads, referring friends, or receiving them from businesses
2. Play Games: Use your doors to play the classic Monty Hall game
3. Win Prizes: Win real rewards from local businesses and redeem them in-store
