# Privacy Policy for OpenDoors

**Last Updated:** November 18, 2025

## Introduction

OpenDoors ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information**: Email address, name, and profile information when you create an account
- **Location Data**: Your location to show nearby games and businesses
- **Usage Data**: Information about how you interact with the app, games played, and rewards earned

### Automatically Collected Information
- Device information (model, operating system, unique device identifiers)
- Log data (IP address, app usage statistics)
- Advertising identifiers for ad serving purposes

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain our services
- Show you relevant games from nearby businesses
- Process and track your rewards
- Send you notifications about plays, rewards, and updates
- Improve our app and user experience
- Serve relevant advertisements
- Communicate with you about your account

## Information Sharing

We may share your information with:
- **Local Businesses**: When you win and claim a reward, we share necessary information with the business to verify your prize
- **Service Providers**: Third-party services that help us operate the app (analytics, hosting, payment processing)
- **Advertising Partners**: For showing relevant ads within the app
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect our rights

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

## Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal information
- Correct inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your account and data
- Opt-out of marketing communications
- Disable location services (though this may limit app functionality)

## Children's Privacy

OpenDoors is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us.

## Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
